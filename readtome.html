Read To Me International

Who we are

Read to Me International Foundation is a nonprofit organization promoting reading aloud to children. The mission and goal is to share the love and joy of reading aloud, esnure that every child is read to every day for at least 10 minutes, model good read aloud techniques and promote reading aloud to a broader audience. Read to Me started as a statewide campaign in Hawaii underscoring the importance of reading aloud with the message of "Every child in Hawaii will be read to every day for 10 minutes". This resulted in the promotion of reading activities and read-along programs statewide and the multi-media campaign having been adopted in over a dozen states, plus Canada and Malaysia.
