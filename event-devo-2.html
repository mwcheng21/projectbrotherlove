Devotional Day 1

"21 And this commandment we have from him: whoever loves God must also love his brother." -1 John 4:21(ESV)


    • Who is your brother/neighbor?
    • Why does God command us to love our "brother/neighbor" as well?
    • What is reconciliation?
    • How do you reconcile with someone?
    • Why do you think it’s hard to love others the way we love ourselves?
    • During this pandemic, were you able to show love to your neighbors? If so, how?
    • What barriers keep you from making loving God the most important commandment in your life?
    • Once we go back to a new normal, do you think those barriers will change?
