Project Brother Love

Who We Are

Project Brother Love (PBL) is a Service and Evangelism project for youth entering 9th grade and up, where youth from churches in the greater Philadelphia area get to serve and love those in need and be a witness to all.

Our Goal

The focus is to expose the youth to urban ministry and the needs in our community. The youth also have an opportunity to share the gospel with those around them and through their experiences at PBL, deepen their relationship with Jesus or begin one.
