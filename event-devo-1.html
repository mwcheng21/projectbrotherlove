Devotional Day 1

"19 We love because he first loved us. 20 If anyone says, “I love God,” and hates his brother, he is a liar; for he who does not love his brother whom he has seen cannot love God whom he has not seen." -1 John 4:19-20(ESV)


    • What is love?
    • What is love in the Christian context?
    • Can you love without action/response?
    • Share a time where you responded with love.
    • Share a time where you DID NOT respond with love. There are times that we all fail to love one another. What causes us to fail at loving others?
    • How can we change our thoughts and actions to help us love others better?
    • What does it mean to be morally right or morally wrong?
    • If we looked at an average day in your life, would we be able to say that it is a good model of love?
