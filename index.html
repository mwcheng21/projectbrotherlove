Important Announcements

  • Please note for the in person days Friday and Saturday:
    • Students must arrive at Chinese Gospel Church (222 N 12th Street, Phila PA 19107) between 8:30- 9:00am EDT
    • For Friday, Delaware participants must arrive at Gospel CAN Church (105 Morrison Ave, New Castle, DE 19720) between 8:30- 9:00am EDT
    • Pick up time on both days will be 4:30pm - 5:00pm EDT
  • All Students will need to:
    • bring their own lunches and water bottles
    • wear closed toed shoes and appropriate clothing
    • wear masks (no other face coverings will be acceptable e.g. scarf)
  • We ask that you do not attend if:
    • you have been feeling any flu like symptoms including including fever, cough, sore throat, headache, weakness, muscle pain, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal pain, or bleeding
    • you have come in contact with or cared for someone with COVID-19
