Helping Hands Rescue Mission

Who we are

Founded in 1905, the Helping Hand Rescue Mission is a faith-based, non-profit organization dedicated to changing lives in the name of Jesus by meeting people at their physical and spiritual points of need. The mission is to be involved with the homeless men and women of the city, to provide for and spiritually counsel them. The mission is also involved with the impoverished citizens that live in the community on the perimeter of the Mission, assisting with food, but also looking at education and other means of impact. Finally, the lives of the neighborhood children are being impacted as the mission ministers with care and counsel to short circuit the cycle that may take these children and produce the next generation of homeless and impoverished. Helping Hand Rescue Mission provides meals for the homeless, runs after school programs for children and a reading program for adults as well as holds weekly sunday service and meals for homeless.
