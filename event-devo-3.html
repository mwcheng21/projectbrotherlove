Devotional Day 3 - I know what you did last summer

“not a result of works, so that no one may boast.”


Is 64:6 "We have all become like one who is unclean, and all our righteous deeds are like a polluted garment. We all fade like a leaf, and our iniquities, like the wind, take us away.""

“Unclean” is not a word we use these days. Perhaps the more modern analogy would be to be infected with the T-virus like a zombie. No one wants to be near you. That’s the way God feels about our sin and us without Jesus Christ. Even if a zombie does something good, it’s still a zombie. That’s the way God feels about our “good works” apart from a relationship with Him.

  1. What good works do you do and why?
  2. Can these or anything else you do save you or make you more likeable to God?
  3. Will you ever be good enough to God?
